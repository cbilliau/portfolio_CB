about

Chris Billiau is an accomplished 7-string jazz guitarist. He has performed with local big bands such as the George Manstan Big Band and String of Pearls. Hailing from the Chicago area, Chris studied guitar under Fareed Haque at Columbia College and jazz composition with Eddie Baker at Roosevelt University.

Though thoroughly comfortable with modern jazz guitar playing, Chris's current work focuses on early pre-swing era guitar.
