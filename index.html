about

Currently a Web Development Career Path student at Thinkful, Chris Billiau has over 20 years of experience in independent management of multiple concurrent, detail-oriented projects. His extensive proven experience in networking and collaborating within small and large work groups has allowed him to excel in both the government and private sector.

A 20 year veteran of the U.S. Coast Guard, Chris Billiau is currently the Reserves Logistics Department Head at Sector Long Island Sound in New Haven, CT.

An accomplished 7-string jazz guitarist, Chris Billiau regularly performs with local big bands such as the George Manstan Big Band and String of Pearls.
